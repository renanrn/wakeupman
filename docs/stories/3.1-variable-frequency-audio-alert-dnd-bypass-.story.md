# Story 3.1: Variable Frequency Audio Alert (DND bypass)

## Status
Approved

## Story
**As a** User,
**I want** to hear a loud alarm even if DND is on,
**so that** I wake up in an emergency.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. AudioManager configuration to bypass DND.
2. Play high-frequency alert sound at MAX volume.
